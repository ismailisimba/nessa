Save Kilimanjaro, The Reforestation Of The Land Surrounding The Mountain

Mount Kilimanjaro, enshrined in our collective memory through its iconic cap of glaciers and the fact that it's the highest mountain in Africa and the highest free-standing mountain in the world, could soon change drastically. Scientific research estimates that its ice could be gone entirely by 2040, leading to a reduction in tourism, water shortages and food shortages.

Nessa is working with the communities in districts surrounding the mountain to plant trees to restore the environment around the mountain. By the start of 2022, Nessa had already facilitated the planting of some 5,000 trees around the region.
Nessa will continue to rally support and work with the community and agencies like TSF to ensure the projects success and mitigate the impacts of climate change to the surrounding area and community.
